User Test Results

I will focus on three issues in user communication today 1. The speed of the picture cycle and the position of the picture text display 2. The style of the title text and the selection of the overall style and color. 3. The overall feeling of using the webpage.

Today, I experienced 3 random user tests. The final feedback suggestion is that the space between the picture content can be more separated or the size of the picture larger, and then only one or two pictures move in the field of view at a time. The position where the text of the picture is displayed can be in the middle of the picture and then the picture is rendered semi-transparent or on the top of the picture, it is convenient to read the information while observing the picture. Then the overall color can be cooler, which can better show the content of the picture. The overall use process is pretty good. But the display of text is missing. Can not fully experience the interaction of the web page.
