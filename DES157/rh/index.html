My direction is to study the visual interaction between web pages or the overall planning of web pages.

Comparison Project 2

This webpage is a webpage with visual effects produced by film producer TAO TAJIMA. Many interactions on the web are very interesting. When you point to a work with the mouse, it will produce a dynamic effect of waves. The general content of the video will be played afterwards. When you scroll the mouse up and down, American works will have a visual effect similar to inertia.

Interaction Strategies

My interaction strategy is that when you enter the website, you will first see the visual interaction. Then guide to some interactions on the mouse.

Project Goals

The goal of my work is to be able to learn some of the advantages of those two websites. Can be well integrated into my own work. Let users experience some different web interactions.

Design Strategies

My work is still as brief as possible. Will not make the web page very complicated. But at a glance, you can know some of the content of the webpage to be expressed.

Experience goals

Allow users to see different visual interactions on the Internet. I will use some cool colors to make my web page.
